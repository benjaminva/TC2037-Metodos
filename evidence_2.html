TC2037 Implementation of Computational Methods

E2 Generating and Cleaning a Regular Grammar

list Instruccions
  • Description:
    Choose a language to generate its Grammar.
    Explain the context of the language.

    Models:
    Generate a grammar that recognizes the language.
    Eliminate Ambiguity in the grammar.
    Eliminate left recursion in the grammar.

    Implementation:
    Implement a lexical analysis using either the automaton or a regular expression.

    Test:
    Implement and document a set of tests, of strings that should be and shouldn't be accepted by the grammar

    Analysis:
    Explain why your grammar is better than other possible grammars
    Explain the time complexity or the number of steps of your solution.
