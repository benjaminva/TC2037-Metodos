E3 Interview for Validation of Understanding

list Instruccions
  • Prepare yourself in advance for the interview:
    Study all of the concepts used in the course.
    Make on the ideas you used in the project, especially the why and the how.
    Upload your notes to e-lumen as evidence that you prepared for the interview before the interview.

    Show up to the interview on time:
    Be 20 min before your time to be safe, not showing up in time will result in failure of the course.
    Answer all the questions asked by the professor about the content of the course as well as your projects
    The time per question is 5 mins. aprox, taking more time will invalidate the answer.

    Examples of possible questions will be in the format:

    1. Explain in detail how you used concept/paradigm A in a piece of code.
      Modify your project/grammar/automaton/re so that instead of doing A it does B.
      Explain why did you use concept/paradigm A instead of B.
      Explain why A is more efficient than B in this piece of code.


    Important:
    Questions will be specifically targeted to each student depending on their previous handins and performance during the course.
    The goal is to have an accurate assesment of the understanding of the student about the concepts of the course and his own handins.

    Failure to answer questions about previously submitted evidences will be reported as FIA.
